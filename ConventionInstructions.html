Below are step by step instructions to help you participate and vote in the Washtenaw County Virtual Convention

Convention: Thursday, December 3rd at 7:00 PM

1) Sign in for the meeting. As you sign in, please identify yourself so that your attendance can be recorded. [There is a place for this in the gotomeetig sign-in dialog.] You can join the meeting from a computer, tablet or smartphone at this URL:

      https://global.gotomeeting.com/join/800968861

You can also dial in using your phone. United States: +1 (408) 650-3123 Access Code: 800-968-861

This will permit you to listen to the convention, but will not enable you to vote.

If you experience difficulty connecting to the meeting please call (734)-709-8766

2) In order to be able to vote, you will also need to log in to the the Washtenaw County Republican Party's December 2020 convention. Please do this early, perhaps right after you sign in to the virtual gotomeeting, so we can help you deal with any issues that might arise.

To get to the voting system, you will need to open up a new tab on your browser and navigate to https://www.wash-gop.com/convention. When you do this, you will see the screen shown here:

3) You will now need to enter the five letter password you were sent in an earlier email into the password box below, and then click on the "Log in to this convention" button.

4) If your login is successful, you will see an introductory screen that looks something like this:

You'll see your name in the upper left hand corner, and then below that two links to elections. The link labeled "Vote in the EC election" will take you to those choices, and the link to "Vote on a resolution" is a poll that we'll use if we need to vote on any other issues at the convention. WE INTEND TO OPEN THE EC Election Poll at 8AM ON THE MORNING OF DECEMBER 3rd. THIS WILL ALLOW US TO "SPREAD OUT" THE VOTING THROUGH THE DAY AND MAKE IT EASIER TO PROCESS ANY HELP REQUESTS WE MIGHT RECEIVE. We will announce during the meeting when the either poll opens or closes.

Below the links to polls, there will be a list of everybody who has logged in to the voting system for the evening. [The names in these instructions are just illustrative. When you log in to the convention, you'll see everybody who has signed in so far, including yourself].

5) When voting for the Executive Committee begins, you will need to click on the "Vote in the EC election" link at which point you will see a screen that looks something like this:

Voting is a TWO STEP PROCESS and this is the FIRST STEP. On this screen, you choose the candidates for whom you wish to vote by clicking on the box to the left of their name. You may select up to 17 candidates (you may vote for fewer, but you may not vote for more than 17). This is analagous to filling out your ballot if you were in a precinct on election day.

6) Once you have filled out your ballot you click on the "Mark my ballot" button at the bottom of the screen.

You'll now see the vote confirmation screen shown here [We've shortened this output considerably and are only showing 5 of the 22 candidates we have as of Monday morning, November 30th. On the night of the convention you'll see all of the candidates for whom anybody has voted.]

On your display, you'll see black bars indicating the candidates you have chosen, and will be given the choice of either accepting the ballot "as is" or going back and re-filling it out. The two buttons at the bottom of the screen implement those choices.

7) Clicking the "Go Back/Revote" button will take you back to the previous screen, and you will be able to re-do your choices. Clicking on the "Confirm this vote" button will tally your vote and take you to a "Thank you for voting" screen. This is analagous to putting your ballot in the tabulator at a precinct and getting an "I voted" sticker.

YOUR VOTE IS NOT COUNTED UNTIL YOU CLICK ON THE "CONFIRM THIS VOTE" BUTTON.

We do not believe that it will take us several days to count the votes in this election, and are fairly confident that we will not have to process truckloads of ballots of unknown and dubious origin deivered at 3AM.

  • If two people are going to use the same computer to vote in the election, the second user will need to "start all over" by going to the https://www.wash-gop.com/convention link and logging in as themselves.
